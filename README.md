1.ORGANISATION OVERVIEW
NAME: REDBAT
BRIEF HISTORY:
REDBAT is popular fashion brand in south Africa, known for offering affordable, trendy clothing and footwear for young people. It is commonly sold through retailers like sport-scene, making it accessible to wide audience. The brand focuses on urban streetwear and casual fashion.
MISSION SATATEMEMT:
To provide stylish, high-quality, and affordable fashion for young,    fashion-conscious individuals.
VISION STATEMENT:
To become one of the leading streetwear fashion brand in south Africa and expand its presence globally.
TARGET AUDIENCET:
. Teenagers and young adults(ages 16-30)
. Fashion-conscious individuals 
. students and young professionals
